Equilibrium Sculpture Lighting System

Project information

  • Client: University of Ottawa
  • Duration: ~35 hrs
  • Skills: Arduino, Infrared Sensors, LED Strips
  • Project URL: Article

Equilibrium is an interactive art installation commissioned for uOttawa’s new STEM Complex. I programmed sensors in one of the building’s stairways to trigger a light show on the sculpture as students walk by. (For fun, I hid a mini-game in the whole system.)
